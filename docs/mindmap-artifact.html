TRACK 01Digital Wealth ManagementBet 3▸ Avatar advisor in the mobile app — AI transacts vanilla, hands regulated products to an RM
Why it can win
  • Whitespace confirmed: no Indian bank ships advise + transact + RM-handoff. BoB "Aditi" (May 2024) is service Q&A only.
  • The hybrid split is the legally correct design: bank = AMFI-ARN distributor → suitability-matched "incidental advice" + execution; insurance/HNI advisory → licensed human.
  • RBI FRSB 8.05% — IDBI is a designated receiving office → the perfect AI-transacts product.
Decided build
  • 2D Rive/Live2D avatar with emotion states (offline-safe, AMA-approved); HeyGen/D-ID photoreal as a toggle.
  • Voice: Sarvam Saarika ASR + Bulbul v3 TTS (won a 20k-vote Indic blind test); Bhashini = free 22-language scale story.
  • Claude on Bedrock in a strict-schema tool loop: get_portfolio recommend execute_SIP create_rm_lead log_suitability.
  • Rules pick the scheme shortlist (auditable); LLM-RAG explains it; live NAVs free via AMFI NAVAll.txt.
  • Account Aggregator mock shows outside-bank MF/NPS holdings (CDSL, NSDL, CRAs are live FIPs).
Compliance spine
  • SEBI Feb 2025: the bank is fully liable for AI output → suitability log + audit JSON + guardrails shown on screen.
  • IRDAI: only licensed Specified Persons solicit insurance → AI does needs-analysis + books the RM.
Demo (7 min)
  • Ravi (mass, Hindi, voice) executes a ₹5k SIP → literacy bite · Meera (HNI) AA holdings → FRSB + insurance RM lead on console · guardrail refuses "guarantee me 15%".
  • Risk: most moving parts of any track — cut to the 3-persona script ruthlessly.
TRACK 02Prospect Assist AIBet 4▸ Behaviour-driven retail-lending leads — interest × repayment capacity, beyond FOIR
The reframe that wins the room
  • No model converts a cold base at 30%. >30% = precision@k on the qualified list handed to RMs — pre-approved leads already convert 20–40%.
  • Funnel economics: "1% means 100 calls per loan; we make it 3." Cost per loan ₹9,800 → ₹700.
Decided build — three visible models
  • Intent (LightGBM + clickstream: EMI-calculator params, session recency) · Capacity (need/want/luxury ledger → surplus → eligible EMI) · Risk gate (soft-pull, zero CIBIL impact).
  • Fusion LQS = P(intent) × capacity_fit × (1−PD) with SHAP reasons an officer can say aloud.
  • Income engines: salary detector · gig payouts (Swiggy/Uber cadence) · UPI turnover × industry-margin priors.
  • Trigger library: rent→EMI pitch, marriage spend cluster, salary jump, broker/property-portal fees.
  • Flex: uplift modelling — target persuadables, Qini curve on the slide.
Compliance spine
  • DPDP: profiling ≠ "legitimate use" → separate marketing-consent toggle + purpose-tagged consent ledger in the UI.
  • RBI Digital Lending Directions 2025: income/occupation assessed, no dark patterns; geolocation coarsened to pincode.
Demo (6 min)
  • Raw statement → categorized ledger → capacity card → intent fires vs window-shopper contrast → risk gate finds a hidden EMI → lead card + RM feedback loop → metrics wall.
TRACK 03MSME Financial Health ScoreBet 5▸ Alternate-data Health Card for credit-invisible MSMEs — explainability mandatory
Positioning
  • The gap: only ~19% of MSME credit demand met formally; ₹69–80 lakh crore unmet.
  • "CMR can't see her. FIT Rank scores her 1–10 in a black box. We hand the underwriter the why" — 7 rails vs FIT's 3.
  • Warning: most crowded track — expect a dozen GST+statement scoring demos.
Decided build
  • 6 pillars (sales, cash-flow, compliance, employment, concentration, utility-verified activity) → composite 300–900 → RAG + go/review/no-go. Never auto-reject.
  • Hybrid two-layer: WoE scorecard (regulator-native) + XGBoost/SHAP challenger; disagreement auto-escalates to the human.
  • Fraud cross-checks before scoring: GST turnover ≠ bank credits · flat kWh vs doubling sales · circular counterparties.
  • Coverage badge answers "SMS alone is not enough" structurally; what-if simulator empowers the underwriter.
  • Rails: GSTN live as AA FIP (mock to ReBIT schema) · Setu/Finvu sandboxes genuinely usable · public GSTIN lookup as the one live API.
Ecosystem numbers for the pitch
  • ULI: 64 lenders · 136+ data services · ₹27,000 cr · AA: ₹1.6 lakh cr loans · GeM Sahay: 7-minute cash-flow loans.
  • FREE-AI 7-sutra → feature table verbatim on a slide.
Demo (6 min)
  • NTC weaver → consent → rails light up → card 668/900 → reason-code drill-down to exact GST filing dates → what-if → stressed Amber case → fraud case auto-flagged.
TRACK 04Default Prediction (EWS)Bet 2▸ 12-month-ahead MSME stress flags on running accounts — RAG buckets in loan-officer language
The framing that wins the room
  • This is a behavioural PD / Early-Warning problem: SMA-0/1/2 ↔ Green/Amber/Red; "12 months ahead" is the IFRS-9 / Ind AS 109 12-month PD → dual-use as SICR staging engine.
  • 16–22% = hit-rate of today's rule-based alerts. The 90% trap: at 5% default rate, "no default for everyone" is 95% accurate.
  • Honest reframe: "~90% of future defaulters already in Red+Amber, at an alert budget you choose."
Decided build
  • LightGBM champion (class-weighted, monotonic constraints) + WoE-scorecard and survival challengers; per-segment isotonic calibration onto one master scale — exactly the brief's "hybrid interpretation".
  • Point-in-time features (≤ T), label = NPA in (T, T+12], out-of-time split — leakage is the credibility killer.
  • Features cite their RBI-EWS ancestors: utilization trend, bounces, inward returns, turnover decline, salary-batch drift, GSTR lapses, e-courts §138, adverse media.
  • Unstructured lane: banker call-memos → LLM flags with verbatim evidence quotes — feature, never decision.
  • Governance-in-the-box: model card + validation report (RBI Aug-2024 model-risk draft), PSI monitors, champion–challenger.
Demo (7 min)
  • Hero moment: "Sharda Fabricators" — zero DPD, Green on today's rules; model says Red · PD 22% · ~8 months to stress; fast-forward shows SMA-2 seven months later.
  • Contrast: seasonal business the rule engine false-flags but the model keeps Green.
  • Numbers: ~85–90% capture at a 20%-of-book alert budget, ~4× lift, calibration curve.
TRACK 05Open Innovation — ReconPilot★ Bet 1▸ Agentic UPI reconciliation + exception-resolution desk — the invited theme, built to spec
Why this is the best bet
  • Mentors explicitly invited reconciliation & KYC — the hidden rubric. The open field will be unfocused chatbots.
  • Scale: UPI hit 23.2B txns / ₹29.9T in May 2026 (~738M/day); banks run 3-way recon: CBS vs switch vs NPCI files.
  • RBI TAT circular monetizes speed: UPI auto-reversal T+1, then ₹100/day automatic compensation — a live penalty clock in the demo.
  • Vendor gap: SmartStream/Cointab/Osfin match; humans still resolve. The agentic exceptions desk is white space.
  • Precedent: MuleHunter.AI — the regulator itself ships ops-AI (23 banks live per RTI).
Decided build
  • S3 → Glue normalization → deterministic match (RRN+amount) → ML fuzzy tail → DynamoDB break register with ageing + TAT clocks.
  • Bedrock agent tools: query_cbs query_npci_record draft_adjustment_voucher compute_tat_compensation — reasoning trace persisted, human approves everything.
  • Natural-language rule authoring: ops prose → matching-rule JSON, dry-run tested.
  • Finacle integration: IDBI's verified CBS — file-based day-1, zero core surgery.
  • Roadmap panes: KYCOps copilot (re-KYC wave crests 30 Jun 2026; 95–99% AML false positives; MNRL triage) · CircularSense.
Demo (6 min)
  • 3 mock settlement files, 50 seeded breaks → 99.7% auto-match in seconds → agent resolves 3 breaks live in recon vocabulary (TCC/RET, suspense GL) → supervisor approves → FTE-hours + penalty-₹ saved.
  • Close: "works on your real files in a 2-week PoC."

Say these in any track
  • Human-in-the-loop architected (override queues, feedback retraining) — not asserted.
  • Explainability three ways: model (scorecard/SHAP) · decision (reason codes) · narrative (LLM phrases, never decides).
  • Multi-source cross-validation — the AMA's "SMS alone is not enough".
  • ₹-quantified ROI on every slide · "IDBI has no AI in production" → be the credible first: governed, auditable, fallback-equipped.
Regulatory spine
  • RBI FREE-AI (13 Aug 2025): 7 sutras — Trust · People First · Innovation · Fairness · Accountability · Explainability · Resilience — mapped to features.
  • DPDP Rules (notified 14 Nov 2025, phased to May 2027) · RBI model-risk draft (Aug 2024) · Digital Lending Directions 2025.
Build-once assets
  • Synthetic Indian statement/UPI generator (personas, VPA realism, seasonality) — feeds T2/T3/T4, useful everywhere.
  • Bedrock tool-loop skeleton with strict schemas + audit log + Guardrails · scorecard+SHAP pipeline · React dashboard shell.
